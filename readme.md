# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Overview
An end-to-end Data Analytics project analyzing customer shopping behavior using Python, PostgreSQL, SQL, and Power BI.  
The project uncovers insights related to customer spending patterns, subscriptions, discounts, product performance, and revenue trends to support business decision-making.

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify high-revenue customer groups
- Evaluate discount and subscription impact
- Discover top-performing products and categories
- Generate business insights using SQL and Power BI

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Subscribers contributed higher overall revenue
- Repeat customers showed better subscription behavior
- Discounts significantly influenced purchasing decisions
- Certain products depended heavily on discount sales
- Young and middle-age customers generated major revenue
